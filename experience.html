My Life

I was born and raised in Bowling Green, Kentucky. After high school, I moved to Lexington, Kentucky where I managed a small, local coffee shop for years before going to massage therapy school. I took a college course in web design and I loved it. My first project was a (very basic) website for one of my favorite shows at the time, Rescue Me.

Years later I discovered Treehouse and decided to give it a go with a few courses here and there before I took the big plunge and signed up for techdegree.
